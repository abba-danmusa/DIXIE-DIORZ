Privacy Policy – Dixie XII Diorz Foundation

Privacy Policy

The Dixie-Diorz Foundation ("the Foundation") is an Incorporated Trustees organization registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA), Cap C20, Laws of the Federation of Nigeria.

The Foundation is committed to protecting the privacy, dignity, and trust of all individuals who engage with it, including beneficiaries, donors, volunteers, partners, and members of the public.

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

We approach privacy as a trust (amanah). Personal information shared with us is handled carefully, respectfully, and only for purposes consistent with our mission. We do not collect information unnecessarily, and we do not use it in ways that conflict with the expectations of those who provide it.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Information You Voluntarily Provide

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Messages or inquiries submitted through our contact forms
  • Information shared when registering for events, programs, or volunteering

b. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect limited technical information such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site

This information is used only to improve website performance and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information solely for purposes aligned with our work, including:

  • Sharing meaningful updates about our programs, activities, and impact
  • Responding to inquiries or requests
  • Managing events, programs, and volunteer engagement
  • Improving our website and communication
  • Fulfilling legal or regulatory obligations where required

We do not use personal data for commercial advertising or unrelated purposes.

4. Email Communications

If you choose to subscribe to our updates:

  • You will only receive communications that are relevant and meaningful
  • We write sparingly and intentionally
  • You may unsubscribe at any time using the link provided in our emails

We do not send spam, promotional marketing, or third-party advertisements.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or misuse personal information.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website or communications (under strict confidentiality obligations)
  • When required by law, regulation, or lawful request by authorities

In all cases, we seek to minimize disclosure and protect individual privacy.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Loss or misuse
  • Alteration or disclosure

While no system is entirely secure, we strive to use best practices to safeguard the information entrusted to us.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
  • Meet legal, regulatory, or reporting requirements

When information is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Children's Privacy

The Dixie-Diorz Foundation does not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent from parents or guardians. If we become aware that such information has been collected unintentionally, we will take steps to remove it promptly.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time

Requests can be made by contacting us using the details below.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites and encourage users to review their privacy policies separately.

11. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, including applicable provisions of the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA).

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

13. Trustees and Contact Information

All matters relating to privacy and data protection may be addressed to the Trustees of the Foundation via:

Dixie-Diorz Foundation (Incorporated Trustees)
Email: contact@dixiediorzfoundation.org
Location: Nigeria

This Privacy Policy reflects our belief that trust is earned through care, transparency, and responsibility.
