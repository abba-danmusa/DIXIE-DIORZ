Our Story

From shared loss to shared purpose.

Learn more about us

We began as boys in blue trousers and white shirts — shaped by discipline we did not yet understand. What once felt strict, life later revealed as formative.

Years after leaving school, loss brought clarity. The passing of one of our own, Auwal Ahmad Aliyu, awakened a deeper sense of responsibility. In grief, we found purpose — and in faith, direction.

The Dixie-Diorz Foundation was born from that moment: a commitment to serve, to uplift, and to carry forward the values that shaped us — quietly, sincerely, and together.

Humanitarian Support

We extend compassion to families facing hardship, providing food, essentials, and relief. Because dignity belongs to every person, and kindness given at the right moment can change a life.

Community Growth

We keep the bonds of the Class of 2012 alive, turning shared memories into collective action. Together, we support one another and give back to the community that raised us.

Emergency Assistance

Life happens to all of us. When a member of our family faces hardship, we show up with love, support, and resources — because community means never facing storms alone.

Annual Outreach

Once a year, we come together — alumni, volunteers, friends — to give back in the loudest, brightest way. A day of unity, compassion, and lives touched forever.

Honouring a Legacy

The values that shaped us.

Professor Ibrahim Alqali Ayagi (late) was more than a Chairman to us. He was a builder of character, a disciplinarian with vision, and a quiet force behind the lives we now live.

Through Hassan Ibrahim Gwarzo Secondary School, he created something rare — an environment where Western education stood firmly beside Islamic values, and where discipline was not punishment, but preparation.

As students, we often resisted his strictness. As adults, we understand its gift.

The work we do today carries echoes of his vision — integrity, responsibility, and service to humanity. In honouring his legacy, we recommit ourselves to the values that shaped us, and to the lives still waiting to be shaped.

Every act of service we offer is rooted in the lessons he left behind.

Our Commitment

Responsible Governance

The Foundation is guided by a duly appointed Board of Trustees and an Executive Committee that provide oversight, direction, and accountability in all decisions and activities.

Ethical Stewardship of Funds

Every donation, contribution, and resource entrusted to us is used carefully and purposefully — directed toward the mission, guided by need, and managed with integrity.

Legal and Regulatory Compliance

The Dixie-Diorz Foundation is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) and operates fully within applicable laws and regulatory requirements in Nigeria.

Open Reporting

We believe trust is strengthened through openness. Our activities, programs, and use of funds are documented and communicated regularly to members, partners, and supporters.
